About MIRA

MIRA is an AI-powered doctor consultant that reimagines digital healthcare through emotionally intelligent, lifelike interactions. Combining natural language processing and a pre-recorded, animated doctor, MIRA engages users in meaningful, accurate consultations.

Every interaction—from initial symptom check to diagnosis—is designed to feel like a real conversation, providing warmth and professionalism.

Our mission is to bridge technology and empathy to deliver accessible, intelligent care to everyone, everywhere.
